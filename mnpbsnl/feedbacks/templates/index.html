Welcome to East Zone MNP feedback portal

This portal provides details of East Zone GSM customer who have generated Port-Out UPC. BSNL concerned of East Zone circles and Call center can take feedback from these customers and then update the same in this portal.

Following information are available in this portal:-

UPC report: Count of PO UPC MSISDNs can be generated in report and can be plotted also - Circle-wise and SSA-wise for the range of selected PO UPC dates.

Feedback report: Count of Feedback completed and pending can be generated in report and can be plotted also - Circle-wise and SSA-wise for the range of selected PO UPC dates. Reason of port-out reason as informed by PO UPC generated customers can be checked and can be plotted too.

Details of MSISDNs can be checked and Feedback can be updated from customers through the options under 'Update Feedback':-

By Circle: Circle-wise details can be checked and updated.

By SSA: SSA-wise details can be checked and updated.

By MSISDN: Particular MSISDN can also be checked and updated.
